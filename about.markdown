Chasing Theta is a personal blog by me, Michelle, which serves to share knowledge about various topics of interest.

The "Theta" in "Chasing Theta" is two things: θ, a letter of the Greek alphabet, and θ, used to denote the statistical parameter used in (Bayesian probabilities)[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Probability_interpretations]. The Bayesian interpretation of probability represented a paradigm shift in the mathematical expression of probabilistic events (the entirety of the future) and, more broadly, in the understanding of truth.

I can expand further on this topic in a future blog post so as not to bore you here, but wanted to provide the context in which I view θ, as a symbol of humility. θ represents the notion that you should always hold room to question your own beliefs, or a voracious curiosity on the nature of things.

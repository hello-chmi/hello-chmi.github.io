Michelle is a 30-year-old perpetual dilettante who programs for money.

"Chasing theta" is a term coined by Michelle to describe her epistemological views. The "theta" in this instance (the greek letter θ can symbolize a wide variety of mathematical constants) represents the statistical parameter used in writing the likelihood function, L(θ|x) = f(x|θ), from Bayesian statistics.

Chasing Theta is a personal blog by me, Michelle, which serves to share knowledge about various topics of interest.

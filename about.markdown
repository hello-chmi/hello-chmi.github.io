Michelle is a 30-year-old perpetual dilettante who programs for money.

"Chasing theta" is a term coined by Michelle to describe her epistemological views. The theta referred to by this term (the greek letter θ can symbolize a wide variety of mathematical constants) represents the statistical parameter used in writing the likelihood function, L(θ\|x) = f(x\|θ), used in [Bayesian probabilities](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Probability_interpretations).
